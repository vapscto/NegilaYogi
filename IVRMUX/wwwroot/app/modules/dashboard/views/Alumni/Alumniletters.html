We trust you desire to continue his association with us in future also. Hence we request you to enroll him as a member of the Old Boys' Association which aims at bringing our alumni together by strengthening their network. Please find enclosed a copy of the Rules & Regulations of the Old Boys' Association.

  The Association organizes programmes like Spiritual Retreats, Regional Meets, Sports and Games Meets etc. exclusively for our old Boys'. It brings out a bi-annual newsletter - Voice of Vidyashala Alumni (VOVA) with latest updates about Vidyashala and OBA. The Association has been conducting a city level Inter High School Competition in General Knowledge since 1973.

   The Association offers two types of membership - Patron (Rs.3000/-) and Life Member (RS.1OOO/-) - both being one-time payments only.

  Your boy will be enrolled as a Life Member by adjusting your caution deposit of Rs.1000/- towards the above membership fees. You need not send any amount for this.

  In case, you desire your boy to be enrolled as a patron, you will please send an additional amount of Rs.2000/- in the form of a crossed DD drawn in favour of Sri Ramakrishna Vidyashala, Mysore, with a request letter to enroll him as a patron of the Old Boys' Association.



With best wishes,


Yours in the Lord,
