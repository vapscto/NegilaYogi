CONTINUOUS AND COMPREHENSIVE EVALUATION

1.
Continuous And Comprehensive Evaluation is intended to provide a holistic profile of the learner through assessment of both scholastic and non scholastic aspects of education spread over the total span of instructional time in schools.
2.
Promotion is based on the day-to-day work of the students throughout the year and also on the performance in the terminal examination.

FOR THE PARENTS

1.
The object of the Report Book is to facilitate holistic learning in school in a stress free environment
2.
The Report Book keeps to parents constantly informed of your child's progress at school throughout the year. They are requested to make it a point to see the Record every term and sign it at the place provided for this purpose.
3.
The Report Book will not be issued if fees are in arrears.
4.
Children must preserve this progress Report carefully as it contains valuable records. In case it is lost during the course of the year they will have to pay a fine of Rs.50/-.
5.
I hope this system of recording your child's progress in school will led to a greater understanding and co-operation between the parents and the teachers.
