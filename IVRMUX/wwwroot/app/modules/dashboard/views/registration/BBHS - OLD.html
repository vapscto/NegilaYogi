GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADMISSION


The application form should be duly, filled by the parents and all the vacant spaces to be marked as ‘NA’. Incomplete forms will be rejected.
The following documents should be attached with each application.
a). Two recent passport size photographs of the child, one for application form and one for test / interview card along with Parents photographs.
b). The application form is NOT complete unless the Original Birth Certificate is attached and the Health Certificate duly filled.
c). Health certificate to be completed by the parents in the application.
d). A copy of the Caste/Sub-Caste/Tribe Certificate in case of (SC/ST/OBC) only.
e). In case of NRI Students, copy of passport has to be attached.
f). The completion of this application form and registration of the name is NO GUARANTEE that admission is granted and gives NO entitlement to claim a seat or any other claims.
g). Once the application form is submitted, kindly note the date and time of written test / Interview from the office without fail. No change of date / time will be entertained.
h). Mobile numbers have been mentioned in the application to facilitate communication with the parents regards to announcements during the admission and though out the academic year.
i). Both the parents are to accompany the child for the interview. In case of single parent or in the absence of both parents the guardians can accompany the child for interview/written test.
j). List of candidates selected for admission will be announced on the school Notice Board.
k). The decision of the selection committee is final.
l). Dealing with any unauthorized person for the admission will entail cancellation of admission.
m). All payments must be made at the school fees counter, Online, Kiosk.
n). Fees Paid is non-refundable.
o). The school being a religious minority institution, we expect our stakeholders to abide by the values instilled by the institution.
p). UNDERTAKING: The school will be sending you information through SMS/E-Mail. We would like you to authorize deployment of the same
q). "No groupism and parents who need to meet the Principal / Manager will meet with prior appointment".
