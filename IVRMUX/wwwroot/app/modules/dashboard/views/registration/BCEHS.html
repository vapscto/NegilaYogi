The application form should be duly filled by the parents and all the vacant spaces to be marked as 'NA. In Complete form will be rejected
The following documents should be attached with each application.
a). Two passport size photographs of the child, one for application form and one for test / interview Card along with parents photographs.
b). Photostat copy of birth certificate, challan / receipt for proof of payment to school office for purchase of application.
c). A copy of the Cast/Sub Caste Certificate in case of (SC/ST/OBC) only.
d). In case of NRI students, copy of Passport.
e). The completion of the this application form and registration of the name is NO GUARANTEE that Admission is granted and give NO entitlement to claima Seator any other claims.
f). Once the application form is submitted, kindly note the date and time of written test/interview from the Office without fail. No change of date/time will be entertained.
g). Mobile numbers have been mentioned in the application to facilitate communication with the parents regard to announcements during the admission and throughout the academic year.
h). Both the parents are to accompany the child for the interview. In case of single parent or in the absence of both parents the guardians can accompany the child for interview / written test.
i). Names of selected candidates will be announced after the interview/test by the School office.
j). The decision of the Selection Committee is final.
k). Donations are not accepted in any form whatsoever, any such attempts to procure admission will entail rejection of the application
l). Dealing with any unauthorised person for admission will entail cancellation of admission.
m). Fees paid is non-refundable.
n). The School being a religious minority institution, we hereby agree to abide by the values instilled by this institution.
